About the Adrian

Greatness, Sustainability, Legacy

At Adrian, education delivers a customized syllabus specifically framed from the scratch which met the local need, satisfying the Global parameters. There is constant effort to raise a happy generation, as we feel that happy children are the most-productive and healthier, whilst we emphasize and work to provide a well-balanced schooling scenario with co-curricular, extra-curricular and academic excellence.

The education system at Adrian is critically analyzed, discussed, researched on and joined by the parents who want not just a Mediocre education that provides only Academic quotients, but caters a Value Based Education for Greatness, Sustaining to set Legacy. Every Adrian is sown with seeds of Greatness to rise up to a Greatness Community.

Schools shoulder an important responsibility of ensuring the holistic development of children. The Adrian schools in Karur is committed to delivering “Best-in-Class” education to its wards across the social and economic Spectrum of Karur.

OUR PRINCIPAL

Our Founder Principal Ms.Janet Ragupathy has over and about 16 years of experience in Educating the Graduates and Post Graduates before which she worked for different corporates like Texas Instruments Inc India Pvt. and Aptech. She has inspired many Graduates to reach great horizons in their lives by recognizing, respecting and nurturing their potential and skills. Ms.Janet was born and brought up at Karur and received her Post Graduation in Computer Applications from Bishop Heber College, Trichy. She has also done her Masters in Philosophy(Computer Science) followed by an M.A in English Literature for her love towards Literature. She loves to inspire and encourage students here at Adrian to be the best they can be in and out of the classroom. She carries with her excellent academic credentials and is a dedicated educationist who thrives to provide a holistic development to the children at Adrian. She demonstrates leadership by example and Motivates staff & students to work to their potential at all times. Her forte` includes teaching with passion and goes beyond books with Practical Application and Cognitive Ignition. She’s a seasoned educator with a broad spectrum of skills and vision

History

Our Path

Since our inception in the year 2014, Our Paramount goal has been to impact the most exquisite education “Sowing the seeds of Greatness” for the foundation education by Mr.K.P.Ragupathy, along with Ms.Janet Ragupathy, Professor in Computer Science for a decade plus. From a humble beginning as ‘The Adrian Kids Nursery & Primary School’, it has now emerged gloriously into an expanded campus, extending from foundation school to Grade X as ‘The Adrian Loyal School’, situated at Madurai bye-pass road, to provide an Authentic Greatness Education at Karur. For the Academic Year 2019-20, the city campus opens its admissions for the children of 2 to 6 years for the Grades Adrian Playgroup to Adrian Senior, whilst the Adrian Loyal Campus is open for Admissions from Adrian Preschool to
Grade X.
