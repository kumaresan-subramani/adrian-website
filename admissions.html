ADMISSIONS @ ADRIAN

As a School providing curriculum that satisfies Global Parameters, we appreciate & acknowledge the flexibility that we ought to possess, and thus, we are open for admissions throughout the year, though the major opening/preference is given from January to April every year.The process of Admission would begin with a campus tour/enquiry followed by receiving the Application form, from either City campus or Loyal School campus Office. However this process alone would not confirm your Admission. Duly filled application form & a token amount must be remitted within 15 days of receiving the Application form to confirm your enrollment. The fee structure does not include any capitation as we work towards Zero Commercialization in our Service.
